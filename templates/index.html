Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors Tournament

Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors! You can play rock paper scissors with a computer by activating the camera sensor below, holding up your hand with a either Rock, Paper, or Scissors, and pressing the space bar to submit. (Tip: the camera best detects what you're holding up when it's just your hand in front of a white background; if you submit while the camera is detecting 'None,' it will return you to the selecting phase to try again.)
